Welcome to Cosmic Cities. Thanks to a recent discovery at the hands of a few talented archaeologists, the world has learned of an ancient civilization that still exists on the backs of three cosmic animals, collectively known as the beasts of Makarim.

Our goal is to introduce these newly found civilizations, give you a dose of their culture, and help you explore their deep and exciting past. Enjoy as we take a dive into the Nile River and travel its currents on top of the cosmic turtle Soleris. Or as we trek through the desert on top of the cosmic camel, Avelis. Also, soar through the night skies on the cosmic owl Verlantis.
