We are the Cosmic Creators, dedicated to preservation and disclosure of the history and creation of Verlantis, Soleris, Avelias, and the desert god. Our goal is to teach people about the desert god and how it created three cities that went through tough circumstances and still survived. The surrounding civilizations believed that all the people from one civilization had disappeared or died. Little did they know that they had found better cities created especially for them. By allowing people the possibility to travel to these cities we are opening a new acquaintanceship between the rest of the world while supporting the distribution of their rich history and how they were able to survive
