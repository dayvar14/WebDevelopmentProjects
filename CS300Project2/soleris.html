Soleris is the name of the cosmic turtle created by Marakim for the distraught citizens of Alexandria. A main control tower located on the shell near the head of Soleris. The richest sector of the city is located nearest to the control tower. As you progress down the back of Soleris, the buildings get progressively smaller and less luxurious. On the sides of the turtle are dock floats that are brought down when the turtle is near the edge of the Nile. These are to allow fishermen to access the waters of the Nile.
