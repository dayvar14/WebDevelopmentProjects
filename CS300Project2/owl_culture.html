Due to their constant scavenging, the people of this city have a diet that is consistent with hunter/gatherers. During the day time, most citizens explore their surroundings and enjoy life outside the city. Due to the owl's nocturnal flights, the nightlife of the citizens is usually spent indoors. Curfews are enforced to prevent anyone from falling off the back of the owl. Due to moving locations constantly, the people never experience problems related to overhunting. This allows citizens to be on the hunt everyday. Hunting is a praised sport and is enjoyed by all who live in Verlantis.
